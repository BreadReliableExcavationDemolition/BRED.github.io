Franz The Great                               21/03/1970
 

     Time to continue the story!!!
     It looked like Shaun didn't care about
     what I was doing, so I kept showing him, 
     hoping that he would understand...
     I mean it doesn't really matter what
     we did for science between the time
     we met and the time it all fell apart...

     We kept making baked goods together,
     one day I cooked for him which was nice!!
     I helped him eat a lot because he said
     he's never felt full before...
     I thought that was pretty depressing,
     honestly, not even as a child????

     Around this time, we had started to
     fall asleep in bed together because
     it was just too far of a travel for
     him to go back to his pharmacy for the
     night...This was at the end of the day
     so we both got rather well rest!!!
        

        

     One day though.. rather, one night,
     I offered he not sleep in my bed, I've 
     got plenty of other beds!! I have an 
     entire medical wing to myself!!! 
     ....I woke up in the morning with Shaun 
     back in my bed....
     Maybe sleeping with another person
     in bed is just a little bit better
     than alone...!! Anyways....
     
     At some point I had been captured by a 
     gang of rogue angel cookies and Shaun had 
     come to my rescue!!....
     Along with a few others I didn't trust
     as much as I trust Shaun...

     Not too long later, a couple days down,
     we made donut hamsters together!! 
     (I think Shaun kept one....)
     They were actually quite delicious!!
     This might have been sometime during 
     Valentine's week because what had happened 
     on Valentine's day has stuck with me since...
