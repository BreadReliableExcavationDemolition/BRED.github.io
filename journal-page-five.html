Franz The Great                               22/03/1970
 

     Time to continue the story I suppose!!
                
     In the conversation where he chose to quit
     the partnership.. 
     he told me that I was distracted
     and that... I am a smart doctor, 
     I will figure it out... I don't rememeber 
     what the IT is...
     I think I was in shock.....

     I left a few days after that, He was right!!!
     I was distracted, and I have big plans that I need
     to do regardless of he will be here for me or not!!!
     
     I set out to find a great witch, 
     I found where she lived through a 
     magazine from "caster's quarterly" that I 
     found in Johnathan's bedroom. 
     Luckily it wasn't filled with pinup women.
  
            

     I traveled far and wide and I eventually 
     did find her!! She was reluctant to hear me 
     out because apparently she's very famous... 
     but I convinced her by flaunting my knowledge 
     of soul surgery!!!
     She was my new partner in the sciences!!!   
                
     She showed me around her house, which was quite
     spacious on the inside, an upper floor, a basement,
     so much room to do... only god knows what!! 
     I also know what happened!! I and that witch!! 
     I do not believe she ever really told me her name,
     but that is fine, I may never see her, or her work
     ever again!!
     Unless I look into a mirror.
     
     It was.... irritating working for her just to learn
     something from her.... all she wanted was a servant..
     But I knew if I kept asking about my magical education,
     she would crack eventually!! And she did!!!
     She eventually taught me a few things, like how to 
     read scrolls and recipes and tomes....
     The first thing I learned how to do was how to 
     cook!!! It was basic chemistry using much different
     ingredients.... I greatly enjoy chemistry!!!
