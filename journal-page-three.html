Franz The Great                               21/03/1970
 

     The day of Valentine's day had mostly gone 
     normal....
     Except Shaun had given me a cute little 
     Valentine's gift!! It was thoughtful of him!!

     They looked cheap and store bought, but I am
     ... was, proud of Shaun for spending some 
     money on something for someone. He's quite
     the cheapskate!!! That's why I always gave
     him food for free before we started bringing
     them to life!!! 
     ............
     I did not have a Valentine's day gift for him.
     I just... did not think about it..
     What adult celebrates Valentine's day anyways???
     Not ones like I whom have better things to focus
     on.... like achieving my goals....!!!

     After I had told him I had not prepared a gift..
     He acted weird for the entire day before I
     finally coaxed him back out of his store so we
     could do some science together!!
            

               One day though.. rather, one night,
               I offered he not sleep in my bed, I've 
               got plenty of other beds!! I have an 
               entire medical wing to myself!!! 
               ....I woke up in the morning with Shaun 
               back in my bed....
               Maybe sleeping with another person
               in bed is just a little bit better
               than alone...!! Anyways....
     
               At some point I had been captured by a 
               gang of rogue angel cookies and Shaun had 
               come to my rescue!!....
               Along with a few others I didn't trust
               as much as I trust Shaun...

               Not too long later, a couple days down,
               we made donut hamsters together!! 
               (I think Shaun kept one....)
               They were actually quite delicious!!
               This might have been sometime during 
               Valentine's week because what had happened 
               on Valentine's day has stuck with me since...
