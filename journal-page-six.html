Franz The Great                               23/03/1970
 

     My new science partner has turned
     out to be useless and cruel... not
     cruel like how Shaun has been...
     more cruel than Shaun...
     although Shaun has told me to stop 
     saying his name recently...
     and we both had a "couple's therapy"
     session with Samson Colbern...
     although we are not a couple, and 
     never have been...
     I am starting to suspect that Shaun
     hates me, more than I could ever be
     upset with him...

     I have sadly posted applications
     for science assistants but I do
     not believe I will truly accept
     another science colleague at all.
     No one could truly replace Shaun...
            

     No matter!!! I will keep exploring
     this new world of science I have
     come across on my vacation!!
     I have been staying up so late in 
     these recent days, testing out
     different ingredients and seeing
     what they do to my bread creatures!!
     (My new bread creatures...
     because a certain soldier I had once
     trusted.. has released all of my older
     creatures....)

     I have created cake lobsters with a 
     real shell by stuffing them with a 
     mixture I like to call....
     the "True Life Serum"!!!!!
     Honestly, it does not give them true
     life.... but it is a fantastic conductor 
     of electricity!!! So it can truly grow
     this time!! and you know... heal itself..
     Maybe....
     I will continue to experiment with this!!
